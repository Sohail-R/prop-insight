# PropInsight

# Finding your future home is messier than it should be. 

Comparing multiple properties across prices, neighborhoods, schools, square footage, and a dozen other factors that shape daily life can quickly become exhausting. The stakes are certainly too high to rush it or get wrong.

# PropInsight was built to ease that burden. 

Whether you're browsing condos downtown, houses in the suburbs, or apartments across multiple cities, we pull it all together for you. No more juggling tabs, no more messy spreadsheets, no more second-guessing what you might have missed.

Powered by Zillow data, PropInsight delivers a clean, side-by-side comparison of every property you're considering: pricing, square footage, mortgage estimates, neighborhood insights, school ratings, walkability, and much more. Now you can spend less time digging and more time deciding.

PropInsight: Buy the RIGHT home, not just the pretty one.
